In my past life, I was a mediocre pure math generalist. I did a little bit of everything, specifically automorphic forms and complex algebraic geometry, but couldn't manage to ship out a single thing in the end. All I ended up doing was writing up a bunch of design docs, coming up with project wikis(“notes”), and strategizing. Ironically, for the very few projects that actually mattered, I just started hustling without design docs, got stuck with inconsistencies, had to call for SOS to rescue me.
My old half baked notes were recently taken down by myself(It surprises me that people actually looked at them). I may or may not come back to them in the future. Most likely not, because I no longer believe in mathematics non-executable by a computer.
